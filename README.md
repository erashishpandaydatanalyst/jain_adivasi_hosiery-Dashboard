# jain_adivasi_hosiery-Dashboard
Based on the dashboard analysis, here is a professional project description suitable for your resume, portfolio, or interview presentation.

## **Business Problem**

Jain Adivasi Hosiery lacked a centralized reporting system to monitor sales, profit, customer performance, product categories, payment methods, and regional sales. Manual reporting made it difficult to identify profitable products, seasonal trends, and areas requiring business improvement.

---

## **Dashboard Goal**

Develop an interactive Power BI dashboard to track key business KPIs, analyze sales and profit trends, evaluate customer and product performance, compare quarterly results, and provide real-time insights for data-driven decision-making.

---

## **Key Visuals**

* KPI Cards: Total Sales (438K), Total Quantity (6K), Total Profit (37K), Average Order Value (121K)
* Monthly Profit Analysis (Column Chart)
* Sales by State (Bar Chart)
* Quantity by Category (Donut Chart)
* Profit by Sub-Category (Horizontal Bar Chart)
* Sales by Customer (Column Chart)
* Quantity by Payment Mode (Donut Chart)
* Quarter Selection Buttons (Q1–Q4) and Interactive Filters/Slicers

---

## **Business Impact & Drive**

* Provided a centralized dashboard to monitor overall business performance in real time.
* Identified Maharashtra as the highest sales-contributing state and Clothing as the leading product category.
* Revealed seasonal profit trends, highlighting profitable and low-performing months for better planning.
* Identified top customers and profitable sub-categories, enabling targeted sales and marketing strategies.
* Analyzed customer payment preferences (COD, UPI, Debit/Credit Card, EMI) to optimize payment options.
* Reduced manual reporting efforts and improved decision-making through interactive dashboards and automated insights.
